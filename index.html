We are the Samah Collective - our mission supersedes world borders. We work covertly & publicly around the world to ensure the preservation and prosperity of humanity. The Samah Collective ensures that threats to the existence of humanity or the normalcy of human society are neutralised or otherwise secured.

The Samah Collective aims to work closely with governments throughout the world in ensuring normalcy is maintained across the world. Due to the highly confidential nature of our work, we expect our officers to act with integrity, respect, and utmost professionalism.

We have three missions : Maintaining normalcy throughout the world, Researching methods to preserve and expand humanity, and enabling governments and international authorities to develop strategies to maintain normalcy within their own borders, independent of the collective.

Our officers are stationed internationally, and therefore we seek to enlist the best and brightest each nation has to offer in our mission to ensure that normalcy can be maintained throughout the world.
